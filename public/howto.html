How to x-up in nemesis:

  1. Fit your ship and link it into incursion chat

  2. Copy the fitting into your clipboard

  3. Paste the fitting into the waitlist tool.

Why do I need to login?

You have to log in via Eve Single-Sign-On in order to protect your own waitlist and to protect your entry on other waitlists. Without the authentication, everybody could delete everybody from waitlists. Or Alice could x-up as Bob. Or a spambot could just kill the entire tool without any way to block him.

What private data do you get from me when I login?

None at all. The only thing I learn is that this is in fact you. The page you enter your login credentials is managed by CCP, I never see your credentials. You can check this by inspecting the URL in your browser, it should look something like the folling example:
